# Survey — Audit Documentation & Workflow Practices

## Status: COMPLETED — Pain confirmed, moving to build.

## Purpose
Validate pain points before building. Framed as "research paper" — no product pitch.
Sent to WhatsApp group (~400 people, Arabic).

---

## Results (2 responses, March 2026)

### Respondent 1 (6-15 person firm)
- Q2: Excel/Word in shared folders
- Q3: Cloud storage (Google Drive, OneDrive)
- Q4: Excel cross-references / hyperlinks
- Q5: Very confident
- Q6: Staff duplicating work because they can't see what's already done
- Q7: 2-5 hours/week wasted
- Q8: Very useful
- Q9: (no answer)
- Q10: (no answer)

### Respondent 2 (6-15 person firm)
- Q2: Excel/Word in shared folders
- Q3: USB/hard drive + in person/printed
- Q4: Excel cross-references / hyperlinks
- Q5: Somewhat confident
- Q6: Difficulty finding supporting documents during review
- Q7: 5-10 hours/week wasted
- Q8: Very useful
- Q9: "Required information not available" (عدم توفر المعلومات المطلوبه)
- Q10: "No" (لا)

### Analysis
- 2/2 use Excel/Word in shared folders (100%)
- 2/2 use manual Excel cross-references for evidence linking (100%)
- 2/2 said tool would be "very useful" (100%)
- 0/2 use CaseWare, AuditFile, or any modern tool
- Combined 7-15 hours/week wasted organizing and searching
- Two complementary pains validated:
  1. No visibility into who did what (staff duplicate work)
  2. Can't find supporting documents when needed
- Sample small (2) but unanimous — combined with founder's 15 years firsthand experience, sufficient to proceed

### Decision: BUILD. Don't wait for more responses.
