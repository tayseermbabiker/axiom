Features

Purpose-built for the audit workflow

Not another project management tool. Axiom is designed around how audit teams actually work.

Trial Balance Import

Upload Excel or CSV files and let Axiom intelligently map your accounts. Full debit/credit support with automatic grouping into audit sections. No more manual copy-paste between workbooks.

Audit Sections & Procedures

Group trial balance lines into logical sections. Define custom audit tests, assign them to team members, and track completion in real time.

Findings Tracker

Log findings with severity levels and status tracking. Attach evidence, record management responses, and follow through to resolution.

Review & Sign-off

Immutable approval workflow. Once a section is signed off, it is locked with a full audit trail of who approved what and when.

Team Collaboration

Role-based access for partners, managers, and staff. Real-time progress dashboards and a detailed activity log so nothing falls through the cracks. Everyone stays aligned without constant check-ins.
